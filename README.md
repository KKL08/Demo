## 🌸 Cyber Anime Pilgrimage (赛博圣地巡礼)

> An AI-powered anime travel photo generator — visit real-world filming locations with your favorite characters, without leaving your seat.

Upload a character image and enter an anime title. The AI will research real-world filming locations (*seichi*) for that series and generate a travel photo of your character at the actual location.

**Key Features**
- 🗺️ AI location research — finds real-world filming locations and background stories
- 📸 Cross-dimension photo generation — blends 2D characters into real-world scenery
- 💾 Local history — saves your last 5 pilgrimages
- ⬇️ One-click download
- 🔐 Privacy-first — your API key never leaves your browser

**Requirements:** Google Gemini API Key ([get one free](https://aistudio.google.com/apikey))
